Business Station

Brief Rebuild this goverment funded business advisory service’s brand from the ground up.Solution The core of this brand was about putting businesses on the right path to success through training and community. The name sums up the concept nicely, and drawing from transportation system and a utilitarian design philosophy, a new visual identity has taken shape. Two highlights of this identity is the splitflap animation which permeates both online and print collateral, as well as the custom typeface inspired by bitmap ticket letters.
